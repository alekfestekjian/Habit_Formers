Habit Formers

Habit Formers is a self-accountability platform designed to help you form good habits, keep commitments, and reach your goals.

There are three stages of the habit forming cycle:

  • The Cue
  • The Action
  • The Reward

Habit Formers is designed to ensure your habits get the cue to restart the cycle so you stay on track. Eventually the cues will come naturally but many people have trouble getting to that stage alone. We also keep track of your accomplishments for you, so you know when to celebrate!
